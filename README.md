

# The Pulik — A Life in Cords and Chaos

**A personal site by Natalie Walker**
Artist · Breeder · Twenty-Six Years with the Hungarian Pulik

---

## About this site

This is the personal website of Natalie Walker — artist, embroidery designer, and devoted Pulik owner for over twenty-six years. The site serves as both an introduction to the Hungarian Pulik breed and a portrait of the life lived alongside them.

It is written in the first person, in the tradition of Anaïs Nin and Marguerite Duras — interior, lyrical, and precise. The Pulik are Natalie's muse. They are part of her soul. This site is an attempt to show both things at once: the breed, and the life that loves it.

---

## What the site covers

- **The Breed** — life stages, history, character and temperament
- **The Craft** — grooming, BARF nutrition, photo gallery
- **The Community** — Hall of Champions, breeders directory, Puli Club of America, show calendar
- **Our Line** — breeding philosophy, bloodlines, what makes this line different
- **FAQ** — honest answers to the questions people actually ask
- **Natalie Walker** — biography, lineage of all dogs, contact form
- **Closing** — a final dedication to Venus Morningstar, Shiva, Califa, and Tsunami
